My games from GAME 601 - Game Prototyping at American University in Fall 2019

Game Modification: PhAJANGO;
A spin on an old dice game. Roll dice for number combinations to fill up your board before your competitor. This new game relies heavily on math and chance. Be able to plan out moves before you run out of them.
PLAY
(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Genesis
Craft a world though cards and chance. Become the gods of your own world. Win by expertly mapping out a world that gives you the most points based on adjacency and terrain combinations. Combine cards together to create different terrain and place them on your board; be careful natural disasters can hit at any time.
PLAY
Interactive Fiction: The Festival
A role playing game in the form of interactive fiction. You are placed in a strange city and must navigate a maze and discover who the true foe is of this world. This small snippet is part of a much larger project that I am working on. Painting done by myself.
PLAY
(Final) Digital Game: ALONE
A hyperlink fiction game about depression, agoraphobia and dealing with loss. This game has around four different endings in the form of poems the author has written in her own personal journey with mental illness. This game is a never ending loop with each ending guiding the user back to the start. Painting done by myself.
